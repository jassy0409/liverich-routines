Upsell Flow Scripts

The masses open the window — these flows walk through it. Set: Red Lace Bedroom, never sold, nobody owns it.
💬

Fan asks about her day"honestly? lazy sunday. new lace came in and i've been living in it. that's the whole story… unless you want the visual"

Tease replies (1pm / 1am, 2 pics attached)

Fan reacts to the pics"told you. and that's the tame angle… there's a whole set from today that literally nobody has. not one person. want to be the first?"
Fan asks how much"depends how much of it you want 😏 tell me what you liked about it first and i'll make it worth it"

Turn-on replies (5pm / 5am, 1 pic attached)

Fan bites"finally, someone with taste. i saved the best ones from this set… the ones i almost didn't take. those don't go on the feed, ever"
Fan buys → keep the door open"you're officially the only one who has these. remember that when i post the boring version tomorrow 🍒 and there's more where that came from when you're ready"
🧭

Read the room

Chatty but not buying? That's fine today — retention is the goal. Warm conversation now is next week's sale.
Satisfied buyer? Warmth, not another pitch. One sale plus a good feeling beats two pushes and a mute.
