The Chatter Playbook · Module 1

Mastering the Comeback

Turning "no" into the next conversation. Don't memorize scripts — learn to understand why the fan is saying no. Every objection gives you information.

⭐️

Before you start

Your job is to identify the obstacle and keep the conversation moving. There are only four obstacles: price, timing, content, and stalling. Learn to name which one you're looking at before you type.

💰 Price objections

"That's too expensive."

What he usually means

"I like it, but not at this price." He replied instead of ignoring you — that's interest.

Fanthat's expensive
✕ Don'tokay babe ❤️
✓ Betteri totally get it ❤️ that's my biggest bundle. i have something smaller i think you'd enjoy just as much

🎯 Goal — find another path to yes.

⏰ Timing objections

"Maybe later / next Friday."

What he usually means

"I'm interested, just not today." That's a booking, not a rejection.

Fani'll buy it next friday
✕ Don'tokay.
✓ Betterperfect ❤️ i'll save it for you. message me friday and i'll have it ready

🎯 Goal — book the next opportunity. Ask when payday is and note it.

🎥 Content objections

"I already have something like that."

What he usually means

"Show me something different." He's not rejecting spending — just that content.

Fani already bought something like that
✕ Don'ttrust me, this one's different
✓ Betterwhat are you in the mood to see today? i'll find something you don't have yet ❤️

🎯 Goal — find what he really wants. Ask before the next PPV.

💬 Stalling

"Haha maybe 😜"

What he usually means

He's enjoying the chat but not moving. You have to lead.

Fanhaha maybe
✕ Don't(chat for 30 more minutes with no ask)
✓ Betterno pressure ❤️ i just thought you'd enjoy this one. want me to send it over?

🎯 Goal — lead the conversation. If he still doesn't move, exit politely and keep it warm.

Common mistakes

  • 1Ending the conversation after the first objection.
  • 2Discounting immediately without understanding the problem.
  • 3Sending another PPV without addressing the objection.
  • 4Assuming instead of asking questions.
  • 5Forgetting to follow up.

Golden rules

  • ★Every objection is information.
  • ★Don't react. Understand first.
  • ★Every reply should move the conversation forward.
  • ★Ask before assuming.
  • ★Always leave another path to yes.
