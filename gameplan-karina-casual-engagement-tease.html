Chatter Rules — All Shifts

The non-negotiables for how every chatter works this casual day, on both pages.
📏

Do

  • Personalize every reply. Use what they said and their name — never copy-paste the same line to everyone.
  • Engagement window = relationship only. On Mass 1 replies, ask questions, react, build rapport. No selling yet.
  • Tease window = the upsell window. Mass 2 repliers are warm — flirt, escalate, then offer content in DM.
  • Price to the fan in DM. Read their spend history and interest, then quote a custom price privately.
  • Tag buyers immediately. When a fan buys, add them to that page's buyers-exclude list so later teases skip them.
  • Log warm leads at handoff. Note who was hot but didn't buy so the next shift can pick it back up.
⛔

Don't

  • No prices in any mass. Ever. Prices live in DMs only.
  • No paid masses today. Every send is free — engagement or tease.
  • Don't hard-sell in the engagement window. It kills the connection vibe.
  • Don't mass the whales/ballers. They're excluded — they get personal 1:1 outreach.
  • Don't reuse a tease photo across pages — Free and VIP have separate assigned shots.

Script Flow — Per Shift, Per Chatter

The same repeatable loop each chatter runs on their shift. Send Mass 1, work the replies, send Mass 2 three hours in, then convert the warm repliers in DM.
🎬

The shift loop

1
Shift open — send Mass 1 (engagement)
Fire the engagement caption for your shift to all fans (minus exclusions). No media, no price.
2
Work the replies — build rapport
React to each answer, ask a follow-up. "aww i love that 🙈 okay tell me one more thing — what are you up to the rest of the day?" Learn one detail you can call back later.
3
+3 hours — send Mass 2 (tease + CTA)
Fire the tease caption + its assigned photo. The CTA already asks them to reply ("tell me your next move").
4
Work the tease replies — escalate
"mmm i was hoping you'd say that 😏 you want me to show you what happens next?" Match their energy, keep it playful, get a clear yes.
5
Convert in DM — price to the fan
"i have something i've been saving… i'll make it just for you 🥵" Quote a private price based on their history. Close, then tag them into the buyers-exclude list.
6
Shift close — handoff
Log warm-but-unclosed fans and any customs promised so the next chatter continues the thread.
💬
