How to run a module

1Run the training session with the Trainer's Guide (intro script, coach's notes, demos).
2Send the Chatter Edition to the team to study.
3Send each chatter their personal exam link (from the chatter hub — links carry their name and Slack routing).
4Scores land in Slack automatically; grade written answers with the Answer Key and coach the weakest point.
