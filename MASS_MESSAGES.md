# Mass Message Idea Bank — bait openers, follow-ups, PPV caption rules

Adapted from the "Writing good mass messages" playbook, rewritten to house
voices. Everything here is a **pattern bank**, not paste-ready copy: no
caption may repeat across gameplans, so rewrite from the pattern every send.

## House voice rules (apply to every line below)

- **Shantal** — proper sentences, first names (`{fan_name}`), warm. Never
  "hey", no pet names, no explicit anatomy terms. Classy over blunt.
- **Karina VIP** — lowercase, playful/bratty. Pet names only for a VIP
  actively spending in the moment.
- **Karina Free** — lowercase, softer and sweeter than VIP.
- Emojis at line ends only. No dashes in fan-facing copy. No "thinking
  about you" on a mass. No guilt-tripping, no needy re-pokes.

## The framework

1. **Bait opener** — a short question engineered to pull the same reply
   ("what?", "go on", "sure") out of everybody. It never reveals the
   payload. This is the free mass.
2. **Follow-up** — pre-written before the bait goes out, lands the moment a
   fan bites. Keep 2 to 3 options per bait so parallel threads don't get
   identical copy. Follow-ups can be a confession, a scenario, or a playful
   dare. Random specific details (what she filmed, what she's wearing, what
   went wrong on set) read as real and carry the thread.
3. **The sell happens in-thread**, matched to that fan, after the reply.
   The bait mass itself sells nothing.
4. Bait masses go to **online/active fans only**, and always **exclude
   buyers** of whatever set the follow-up ladders toward.

## The "needy gf energy" play — Karina VIP only

The source doc's "crazy needy gf who demands your attention" angle fits
exactly one voice in the house: Karina VIP's brat. It stays **playful
demanding, never guilt**. Demanding attention as a game is on-voice;
"why are you ignoring me" is a banned needy re-poke.

- opener: `new rule. u owe me attention on demand now 😤`
- follow-up: `i don't make the rules. wait. i literally do. come entertain me 😼`

Do NOT run this angle on Shantal (warm, never demanding) or Karina Free
(softer). Shantal's equivalent energy is the "full attention" framework
above, offered rather than demanded.

## PPV caption rules (paid masses)

The doc's core rule holds: **a PPV needs a theme and a high-effort caption,
and the fan must feel he is missing out by not opening.** House formula:

1. **Name the set** — the vault folder theme is the story ("the gray top
   and green pants video", "the shower collab"). Never a generic drop.
2. **One concrete detail** — length, what almost didn't get filmed, what
   made her blush. Specifics create FOMO; adjectives don't.
3. **Sell the moment, not anatomy** — house-safe wording, classy over
   blunt. Suggest, never list.
4. **One reason to open today** — a real one (comes down tonight, price
   goes up, only buyers of part 1 get part 2 priced like this).
5. **Clear CTA at the end** — every reminder ends on a CTA, and reminders
   never use "unlock" wording.
6. Price per the pinned pricing guide. Exclude the set's previous buyers
   and the universal exclude lists on every send.

## Escalation ladder (in-thread, after a bait or a PPV open)

The doc's escalation script translated to house rules, structure only:

1. **Match, don't lead.** Mirror what the fan introduces and let him set
   the ceiling. The chatter never escalates past where the fan already is.
2. **One step at a time.** Each escalation is a single step up in heat,
   never a jump. A fan at banter gets a tease, not a menu.
3. **The next step lives behind the lock.** When he is leaning in, the
   escalation is a priced send, not a free paragraph. Free lines carry
   tension; content carries the payoff.
4. **Read the done signal.** Satisfied fan = warm close and a reason to
   come back tomorrow. Over-pushing past a done signal is a dead-end flag
   on the scorecard.

## Bumps (quiet-board plays)

Cadence: if nobody is chatting, one bump at the top of the hour max, and
**unsend the previous bump when a new one goes out** (free sends only, the
paid mass always stays). Online/active fans only, exclude buyers of
whatever the bump ladders toward. A bump is an invitation, never a chase.

## Objection handling (honest versions only)

The source scripts for these situations lean on fake excuses, false
promises, and guilt. All three are banned here. House versions:

- **Wants to meet / move off platform** — warm no, stay on platform,
  never dangle meeting as something spending could buy.
  - Shantal: That is sweet of you, but I keep everything here. This is where I can actually give you my time and attention.
  - Karina: i only hang out here, that's the rule. lucky for u i'm very fun here 😏
- **Says it's expensive / can't afford it right now** — zero guilt, leave
  the door open. If he is still leaning in, offer one smaller bridge, then
  let it rest.
  - Shantal: No rush at all, {fan_name}. I am not going anywhere, so we can pick this right back up when the timing works for you.
  - Karina: all good, it'll still be here when u are. don't be a stranger 🤍
- **PPV sitting unopened** — one warm nudge per shift per fan, with a
  concrete hook, never "unlock" wording and never a bare "you there?".
  - Shantal: The best part of what I sent is still waiting for you, and I would hate for you to miss it tonight.
  - Karina: that thing i sent u is aging like fine wine. open it before i get shy 😏
- **Duplicate send / he already owns it** — honest apology, make it right
  with something he does not have. Never blame platform glitches.
  - Shantal: You are right and that one is on me. Let me make it up to you with something you have not seen.
- **Paid but never received, wrong item, any billing dispute** — NO
  script exists for this on purpose. Freeze the thread, no offers, no
  selling, escalate to Jassy and the model per the compliance protocol.
- **Timewaster** — polite exit, no insult, no "inbox full of guys" guilt.
  - Shantal: I love chatting with you, but my time here has to go to the people who support me. I will check back in another time 😊
  - Karina: ur fun but i gotta go earn. behave while i'm gone 😏
- **Card problem** — patience, no pressure.
  - Shantal: Sort it out whenever you can and come find me after. I am not going anywhere.

## Aftercare (post-purchase warm close)

Every meaningful purchase ends with a warm close and a **booked** return
hook, not an open goodbye. Name the next thing and when. No withholding
after a purchase, and no selling in the aftercare line itself.

| Voice | Example |
|---|---|
| Shantal | I loved that more than I planned to admit. Come find me tomorrow, I want to hear how the rest of your night went 🌙 |
| Karina VIP | ok that was fun. ur officially my problem now. same time tomorrow 😼 |
| Karina Free | that honestly made my whole night. come say hi tomorrow ok 🫶 |

The house VIP program is real, so every pitched perk must be one we
deliver: priority replies, first look at new sets, VIP-only content, the
custom name treatment. Price per the pinned guide. No invented countdown
discounts, no "boyfriend status" product (the gf/bf experience is a chat
style here, not a purchasable tier). **Loop Jassy in before quoting any
SVIP number.**

| Voice | Example |
|---|---|

## Replying to explicit fan photos

Acknowledge playfully, stay inside the voice ceiling no matter what he
sent, and move the escalation behind the lock instead of matching him in
free text. One tease line, then the priced next step. If it crosses into
something that needs a decision, that is an escalation, not a chat.
