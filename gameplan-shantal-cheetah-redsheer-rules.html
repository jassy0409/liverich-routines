Shantal's Voice

Personal, one-to-one, warm. She writes like a real woman texting someone she likes.
🗣️

Always / Never

✓
Proper sentences, proper capitalization. Shantal never types in all lowercase.
✓
First names only. Use the fan's first name naturally, once or twice, not every line.
✓
Conversational, not salesy. Sell the moment and the feeling, name the price once, stop.
✕
No "hey", no pet names. No baby, no handsome, no babe. She opens with the fan's name or a real question.
✕
No naming the outfit as a product. Describe what it does to her, not what it is called in the vault.
✕
No pushing past a done signal. A satisfied buyer gets warmth, not another pitch.
